Meet Samuel Bookman

Founder & Lead Immigration Attorney

Samuel Bookman is the founder and principal attorney of Crest Immigration Law Firm. With over 15 years of experience in immigration law, Samuel has dedicated his career to helping individuals and families navigate the complex world of immigration.

Born to immigrant parents, Samuel understands firsthand the challenges and opportunities that come with building a new life in a new country. This personal connection to the immigrant experience drives his passion for providing compassionate, effective legal representation to every client.

Samuel earned his Juris Doctor from a prestigious law school and has been recognized for his expertise in immigration law by numerous professional organizations. He stays current with the ever-changing immigration regulations and policies to provide the most up-to-date advice to his clients.

Our Firm's History

Crest Immigration Law Firm was established in 2008 with a clear mission: to provide accessible, professional immigration legal services to individuals and families seeking to build their future. Since our founding, we have successfully helped hundreds of clients achieve their immigration goals, from obtaining work visas to securing citizenship.

Our firm has grown from a solo practice to a respected name in immigration law, known for our attention to detail, personalized service, and unwavering commitment to client success. We pride ourselves on staying at the forefront of immigration law developments and utilizing the latest legal strategies to benefit our clients.
